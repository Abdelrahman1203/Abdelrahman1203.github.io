# Eastern & Co — Law Firm ERP/CRM

## One-line summary
Modular Laravel ERP/CRM for **Eastern & Co** law firm — public bilingual website plus admin for CRM, legal tasks, HR, finance, and CMS.

## Tech stack
- **Backend:** Laravel 10, PHP 8.1+, MySQL, nwidart/laravel-modules (8 modules)
- **Auth & RBAC:** Spatie Permission, session-based Employee guard
- **Frontend:** Blade, Vite, Laravel Mix, legacy admin assets
- **Integrations:** Twilio SMS/WhatsApp, reCAPTCHA, Maatwebsite Excel, Intervention Image
- **i18n:** English + Arabic (Africa/Cairo timezone)

## Key features
- **Website:** Public law firm site (services, cases, attorneys, blog, careers)
- **Sales:** Lead CRM, follow-ups, bulk Excel import, SMS, analytics
- **Lawyer:** Legal task workflow (manager/lawyer/finance views)
- **Finance:** Invoices (EN/AR print), expenses, safes, petty cash
- **HR:** Employees, vacations, job applications, recruitment reports
- **CMS:** Pages, menus, slideshow, blog, testimonials

## CV bullet points
- Delivered a modular Laravel 10 ERP/CRM for **Eastern & Co** law firm with lead CRM, legal task workflows, HR, and invoicing across 8 modules.
- Implemented Spatie RBAC, bilingual EN/AR UI, Twilio SMS integration, and Excel import/export for lead management.
- Built public marketing website and permission-gated admin for clients, legal tasks, and financial operations.
